About

Who am I?

Hello! I’m your friendly neighborhood Christoffer, and I’m absolutely delighted to welcome you to my little corner of the internet.

Originally from Gothenburg, I’ve spent countless hours immersed in the fascinating worlds of gaming and coding. Whether I’m crafting elegant software solutions, savoring a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, or embarking on epic quests in action role-playing games, I find joy in both the simple pleasures and the intricate challenges.

My path has been anything but ordinary. From my days as a humble postman to my current role as a fully-fledged developer, I’ve navigated twists, turns, and unexpected surprises. Each experience has contributed to shaping the person I am today—a blend of curiosity, determination, and a dash of whimsy.

Skills

Frontend

Backend

With extensive experience in backend technologies, I specialize in Java, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and Spring. Proficient in AWS, I design and deploy scalable solutions. My hands-on expertise extends to both SQL databases (like PostgreSQL) and NoSQL databases (including DynamoDB).

Game Development

With comprehensive game development knowledge from academic courses, I bring concepts to life through the Unity game engine, utilizing C# to create engaging gameplay and interactive features. My skills extend to 3D modeling with Blender, adding depth to the visual storytelling of my games. I'm not just a developer; I'm a creator who shapes immersive gaming experiences.

Ethical Hacking

As someone who has delved into the world of Ethical Hacking, I’ve built a robust understanding of critical topics. My knowledge spans network vulnerabilities, website security flaws (including SQL injection and XSS), Spoofing techniques, and Malware. Testing

As an experienced developer, I bring a wealth of knowledge in various testing tools and frameworks. Specifically, I specialize in JUnit for rigorous unit testing, Cucumber for Behavior-Driven Development (BDD), and Gatling for load testing. By leveraging these tools, I ensure maximum code quality and efficiency, effectively mitigating bugs and performance issues.
